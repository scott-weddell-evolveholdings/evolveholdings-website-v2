# The Trades Lab — Offer & Positioning (source of truth)

**Audience:** UK trade businesses (this template = bathroom fitters).

## The offer (approved framing)
NOT a free build. Free = a **bespoke demo we build first**:
> "See your new site before you pay a penny. Love it? It's yours, from £1,500. Don't? Keep it
> for ideas."
- Paid = done-for-you site **from £1,500**, one-off, client owns it outright.
- Scarcity = one site per trade, per town. **No fake "X towns claimed" counters.**
- Drop "free in 2026 / £997 value" language.

## Price-advantage framing
Frame the saving as **systemisation**, NOT "no monthly fees" (the back-end upsell is monthly
Google Ads + SEO, so don't undercut it):
> "Agencies charge £5,000+ because they build from scratch; we've systemised it — from £1,500."

## Proof (no real trade testimonials yet — do NOT fabricate)
Use **founder/team pedigree** (£6.8M+ AUD ad spend managed, 60,000+ customers, 1,000+ campaigns,
10+ yrs), framed as marketing capability — not trade-client results. Any before/after gallery is
a **feature demo**, captions marked "Example". Note: this demo currently contains illustrative
review quotes — for a real client, replace with their genuine reviews or remove.
