What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files that a website can place on your device to make it work properly, remember your preferences, or understand how it's used. Some are essential; others are optional.

We do not currently use advertising cookies, cross-site tracking, or third-party analytics on this site. If that changes, we'll update this page and, where required, ask for your consent first.

Controlling cookies

Most browsers let you see, manage and delete cookies through their settings. Blocking all cookies may affect how some parts of this site display. You can find out more about managing cookies at aboutcookies.org.
