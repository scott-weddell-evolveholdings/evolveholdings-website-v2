06 Common questions

Bathroom renovation FAQs

How much does a new bathroom cost in Manchester?+

Most full bathroom renovations range from a few thousand pounds up to £15,000+, depending on the size of the room, the spec of fittings and the amount of tiling. We give a clear, fixed price within 48 hours so you know exactly what you'll pay before any work begins.

How long does it take to fit a bathroom?+

A typical full bathroom refit takes 5 to 10 working days, depending on the size and complexity. We agree the schedule with you up front and finish on time, leaving the space spotless.

Do you supply the bathroom or do I?+

One team handles everything — design, supply, tiling, plumbing and electrics — so there's no juggling trades or sourcing materials yourself. We can also fit items you've already bought.

Are your bathroom fitters insured and guaranteed?+

Yes. Every fitter is vetted and fully insured, work is Gas Safe registered where required, and every installation is backed by our 10-year workmanship guarantee.

Which areas do you cover?+

We design and fit bathrooms across Manchester and Greater Manchester, including Didsbury, Altrincham, Stockport and the surrounding areas.
