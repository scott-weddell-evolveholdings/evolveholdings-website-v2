Privacy Policy

Last updated: 6 July 2026

Evolve Holdings ("we", "us", "our") provides bathroom design, installation and renovation services in Manchester and Greater Manchester. This policy explains what information we collect through this website, why we collect it, and how we look after it.

1. Who we are

Evolve Holdings is the data controller for the personal information described in this policy. If you have any questions, you can reach us on +44 7911071673 or at hello@evolveholdings.co.uk.

2. Information we collect

We collect information you choose to give us, such as when you submit an enquiry or quote request through this website or contact us by phone. This may include:

  • Your name
  • Your phone number
  • The type of bathroom project you're enquiring about
  • Any other details you share with us about your project

We do not knowingly collect sensitive personal data, financial details, or information from children through this website.

3. How we use your information

We use the information you provide to:

  • Respond to your enquiry and arrange a free quote or consultation
  • Communicate with you about your project, before and during any work
  • Keep records for our own accounting, legal and quality purposes

We will never sell your information to third parties, and we only contact you about the project you asked us about.

4. How long we keep it

We keep enquiry details for as long as reasonably necessary to respond to you and, where you become a client, for the duration of our working relationship plus any period required by UK tax and accounting law. If you ask us to delete your details and we have no ongoing legal reason to keep them, we will.

5. Cookies

This site uses only the minimum technical resources needed to display it correctly, including a font stylesheet served by Google Fonts. See our Cookie Policy for full details. We do not currently use advertising or cross-site tracking cookies.

6. Your rights

Under UK data protection law, you have the right to ask us for a copy of your personal information, to correct it, to delete it, or to restrict or object to how we use it. To exercise any of these rights, contact us using the details above. If you're not happy with how we've handled your data, you can also complain to the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO).

7. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "last updated" date.
