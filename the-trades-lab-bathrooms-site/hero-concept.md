# Hero Concept — Bathrooms demo (high-end UK buyer)

The before/after hero must make an affluent homeowner feel **status**, not just
"clean bathroom." We design the imagery for the plumber's *end customer* — the
person they want to attract.

## Who we're seducing (buyer psychology)

**Who:** Affluent UK homeowner — £1m+ investable or high-earning professional /
business owner. In our Manchester demo, picture the Cheshire "golden triangle"
(Alderley Edge, Wilmslow, Prestbury, Hale): footballers, entrepreneurs, execs.
Homes are period properties (Victorian/Edwardian/Georgian) or premium new-builds &
barn conversions. UK median renovation spend is ~£21k; the top 10% spend £100k+.

**What the bathroom now means to them:** it's a **status symbol + private wellness
sanctuary** — "a five-star spa at home." It signals success, taste, and that
they've made it. The emotional payoff is the friend who walks in and goes *"wow,
this is incredible."* Drivers: pride, status, self-reward, calm/escape, refined
taste, control.

**The "before" pain (what they're embarrassed by):** a tired 1990s/2000s bathroom
— builder-beige or avocado/dusty-pink suite, cramped over-bath shower with a flimsy
screen, cracked grout and mouldy sealant, boxed-in bath, frosted window, swirly
lino, a single harsh ceiling light, limescale. Functional, dated, not something you
show off.

**The "after" dream (2026 luxury):** timeless, not trendy. A **sculptural
freestanding stone/marble-resin bath as the centrepiece** (ideally under a window
or rooflight), a large **frameless walk-in rainfall shower**, **marble/travertine
large-format tiles** with soft veining, **brushed-brass or matte-black** fixtures,
a **floating oak/walnut vanity with a stone basin**, underfloor heating, concealed
clutter-free storage, backlit mirror + warm ambient lighting, natural light and a
plant or two. Hotel-calm. Craftsmanship you can feel.

## Aesthetic north star
"Five-star spa inside a Cheshire period home." Marble + brass + warm natural light +
one sculptural hero piece. Understated, expensive, immaculate. NOT flashy/gaudy.

## Technical
- **Aspect ratio: 16:9 landscape** (website hero, full-viewport). High-res.
- Tool: **Higgsfield → GPT Image (2.0)**.
- Each concept = ONE room + ONE camera angle, with a matching **before** and
  **after** so the scroll-scrub morph (and the before/after slider) lines up.

---

## Concept A — "The Statement Bath" (recommended hero)
A wide, symmetrical master-bathroom shot built around a freestanding bath under a
big window. The single most "wow/status" image — the centrepiece the 2026 trend is
literally designed around.
- **Before:** cramped, dated UK family bathroom, same angle — boxed-in bath under a
  frosted window, beige/cream wall tiles, flimsy over-bath shower screen, oak
  pedestal sink, worn lino, harsh ceiling light, tired grout.
- **After:** serene spa master bathroom — a sculptural matte-stone freestanding bath
  centred beneath a large window with soft morning light, full-height book-matched
  marble wall, floor-standing brushed-brass bath tap, floating oak vanity with stone
  basin, large-format travertine floor, frameless glass to a walk-in rainfall shower
  at the side, warm ambient lighting, a single olive tree. Five-star, immaculate.

## Concept B — "The Walk-In Spa Shower"
Angle on a large frameless walk-in wet-room shower — conveys power + craftsmanship.
- **Before:** small over-bath shower, white plastic, mouldy sealant, flimsy screen,
  beige tiles, cramped.
- **After:** huge frameless walk-in wet room, marble large-format walls, ceiling
  rainfall head + brushed-brass controls, stone bench seat, niche shelf, underfloor
  heating, glowing warm light.

## Concept C — "The His-&-Hers Vanity"
Angle on a double floating vanity — conveys daily luxury + taste.
- **Before:** single pedestal sink, oak cabinet, cluttered, harsh light, dated mirror.
- **After:** floating walnut double vanity, twin stone basins, twin brushed-brass
  taps, backlit mirrors, marble splashback, concealed storage, sconce lighting.

**Recommendation:** lead the hero with **Concept A** (the freestanding-bath
transformation — maximum status wow). Use **B** and **C** in the before/after
gallery so the whole page sells craftsmanship.
